Security camera systems work best when the wiring, equipment placement, and coverage plan are handled together. Polos Electronics installs surveillance systems for residential and commercial properties across Battle Ground, Vancouver, and Southwest Washington.

We help with camera placement, cable routes, recorder location, monitor or app access, and the details that make the finished system easier to use. If you already have a system that is unreliable or messy, we can troubleshoot, organize, and improve the installation.

Our work includes camera cabling, DVR and NVR setup, remote viewing configuration, and wiring cleanup. We focus on practical coverage, clean installation, and systems that make sense for how the property is used.

Polos Electronics has provided low-voltage electrical work since 1979. Call for a free estimate if you need a new camera system or want help improving an existing one.
