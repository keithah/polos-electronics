Home theater and audio systems can become frustrating when the wiring is confusing, components are mismatched, or controls are hard to use. Polos Electronics installs and repairs AV systems with a focus on clean wiring, practical equipment placement, and dependable operation.

We work on home theater rooms, surround sound, whole house audio, TV and receiver connections, and existing systems that need troubleshooting. Many customers call us when a system used to work but no longer does, or when a previous installation left cable clutter behind.

Our team can route and label cables, connect components, help place speakers, and clean up wiring so the system is easier to understand and maintain. We serve homes across Vancouver, Battle Ground, Clark County, and surrounding Southwest Washington communities.
